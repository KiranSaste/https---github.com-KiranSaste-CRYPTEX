About BITUSD

BITUSD is a stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar, providing a stable digital asset for trading and storing value. Each BITUSD is backed by cryptocurrency collateral, ensuring its stability and reliability in the digital asset ecosystem.

Key Features:

  • 1:1 USD Peg
  • Fully Collateralized
  • Instant Transfers
  • Low Transaction Fees
  • Smart Contract Security
